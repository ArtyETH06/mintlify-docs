<Warning>
  Token launches carry real financial risk. Read and understand the following before participating.
</Warning>

## Market Risk

Token prices can drop to near zero. Locked liquidity prevents rugs; it does not protect you from price falling if demand disappears.

## Project Risk

Even with fair launch mechanics, teams can still ship late, fail, or abandon a project.

## Concentration Risk

Per-wallet limits help, but a few large actors can still buy many tickets across multiple wallets.

## Smart-Contract Risk

On-chain systems can have bugs or unforeseen edge cases, even if audited and battle-tested.

## Regulatory and Tax Risk

Rules can change in your country, and you are responsible for your own reporting and compliance.
