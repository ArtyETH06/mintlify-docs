title: "Security & Risk Scoring"
description: "How RevolutionPad evaluates wallet risk and launch health to protect participants."
---

RevolutionPad uses two complementary scoring systems to keep launches fair and transparent.

## Wallet Risk Score (0–100)

Every wallet receives a risk score based on on-chain signals. The score is computed from five categories:

- **Account Age & Origin** — how old the wallet is and where it was funded from
- **Transaction Depth & Diversity** — how many programs the wallet has interacted with
- **Presale Farming Pattern** — repeated claim-and-dump behavior across launches
- **Rug / Scam Interaction** — links to known scam contracts or blacklisted wallets
- **Bot & Cluster Detection** — circular funding, identical buy sizes, coordinated registrations

| Score | Level |
|---|---|
| 0–20 | 🟢 Low Risk |
| 21–50 | 🟡 Monitor |
| 51–75 | 🔴 High Risk |
| 76–100 | ⛔ Severe Risk |

## Launch Health Score

Each launch is also scored based on the wallets participating in it:

- **Concentration risk** — are a few wallets holding most of the tickets?
- **Temporal patterns** — are there signs of bot-driven mass participation?
- **Cluster overlay** — what percentage of tickets come from high-risk wallets?

Both scores are displayed publicly to help investors make informed decisions and to naturally discourage manipulation.
