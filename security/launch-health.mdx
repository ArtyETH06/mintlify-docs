---
title: "Launch Health Score"
description: "How RevolutionPad assesses the distribution risk and overall health of each launch."
---

Every launch on RevolutionPad receives a **Launch Health Score** based on the wallets participating in it. This helps investors gauge the quality and fairness of a launch before committing.

## Concentration Metrics

| Signal | Severity |
|---|---|
| Gini coefficient > 0.85 | 🔴 RED |
| Top 1 wallet > 50% of tickets | ⛔ CRITICAL |
| Top 5 wallets > 80% of tickets | 🔴 HIGH |
| Top 20 wallets > 90% of tickets | 🟡 Monitor |

## Temporal Patterns

- **30+ participations in < 2 minutes** — Bot suspicion flag
- **Abnormal variance in participation timing** — Spike score applied

## Cluster Overlay

If **30%+ of tickets** come from wallets classified as High Risk, the launch is flagged:

```
Launch Health: 68/100
Concentration risk: HIGH
Wallet risk average: Elevated
Cluster detected
```

## How It Helps

- **Investors** can see at a glance whether a launch is dominated by bots or clusters
- **Founders** benefit from higher-quality participant pools
- **The platform** naturally discourages manipulation without requiring manual intervention
