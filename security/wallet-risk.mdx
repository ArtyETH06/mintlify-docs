---
title: "Wallet Risk Score"
description: "How RevolutionPad evaluates wallet risk using on-chain data to detect bots, sybil farms, and bad actors."
---

Every wallet that interacts with RevolutionPad receives a **Wallet Risk Score** from 0 to 100. This score is separate from the internal Investor Rank — it measures external risk signals using on-chain data.

## Purpose

- Detect bots and sybil farms
- Identify wallets linked to rug pulls
- Reduce aggressive farming
- Provide a public trust signal

## Score Categories

The score is computed from five equally weighted categories (20% each).

### A. Account Age & Origin

| Signal | Risk Points |
|---|---|
| Wallet < 7 days old | +25 |
| 7–30 days old | +15 |
| 1–3 months old | +5 |
| 3+ months old | 0 |

<Warning>
  10+ wallets created on the same day from the same funding source is a major red flag.
</Warning>

### B. Transaction Depth & Diversity

| Signal | Risk Points |
|---|---|
| < 10 transactions | +20 |
| < 3 programs used | +10 |
| Only interacts with presales | +15 |
| 50+ programs used | -5 (organic signal) |

### C. Presale Farming Pattern

| Signal | Risk Points |
|---|---|
| 20+ presales in 30 days | +20 |
| Repeated claim + sell < 10 min | +25 |
| Never holds > 24h | +15 |

### D. Rug / Scam Interaction

| Signal | Risk Points |
|---|---|
| Bought a token flagged as scam | +10 |
| Interacted with 3+ rug contracts | +20 |
| Received funds from a blacklisted wallet | +25 |

### E. Bot & Cluster Detection

| Signal | Risk Points |
|---|---|
| 10+ wallets with circular funding | +30 |
| Transactions in the same slot | +30 |
| Identical buy sizes repeated | +30 |
| 20 wallets registered within 30 seconds | +30 |

## Risk Classification

| Score | Level | Label |
|---|---|---|
| 0–20 | 🟢 | Low Risk |
| 21–50 | 🟡 | Monitor |
| 51–75 | 🔴 | High Risk |
| 76–100 | ⛔ | Severe Risk |

## Public Display

On a wallet's profile page:

```
Wallet Risk: 72/100 (HIGH)

Flags:
  3 rug interactions
  Farming pattern detected
  Cluster detected (12 wallets)
```
