---
title: "What is RevolutionPad?"
description: "RevolutionPad is a launchpad on Solana that turns token launches into a simple, rule-based process instead of a click race."
---

RevolutionPad makes token launches predictable: founders lock in clear parameters up front, and the protocol enforces them on‑chain from setup to trading.

Founders set a few key parameters: team share, funding targets, and distribution rules. The protocol runs the ticket sale, checks whether the minimum is met, and, if it is, sends all effective SOL plus a fixed share of tokens into locked liquidity on Meteora. From there, trading starts on a pool that nobody can pull, and founders earn only from trading fees and a long‑term Streamflow vesting of their team allocation.

**For investors**, this means fair access through tickets, clear outcomes (refund, pro‑rata, or raffle when demand exceeds the max cap), and launches that always open on real locked liquidity.

**For founders**, it means less time wiring together DeFi parts and more time building on top of a structure that investors can understand in a few lines.
