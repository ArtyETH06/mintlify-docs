As a participant you do not need to understand every DeFi trick. You need to see that the rules are fixed, the raise logic is clear, and liquidity is locked on Meteora.

## What Each Launch Page Shows

On each launch page you will see:

- **Token**: name, ticker, token team supply.
- **Splits**: team/sale.
- **Sale rules**: ticket price, min cap, max cap, max winning tickets, start/end time.
- **Links** to the Meteora pool and Streamflow vesting once live.

## Reading a Launch Like an Expert

If you are more advanced, you can go one level deeper. You can inspect the contract, caps, and vesting to judge whether the setup is aligned or predatory.

Founders who follow the standard — locked liquidity, long vesting, public identity proofs, and clean splits — are signaling they are here for a real, long-term project, not a quick exit.

By passing all the checks (ownership vouches, sane caps, distributed supply), they show they have done what you should expect from a legitimate founder: no hidden mint keys, no honeypot tricks, no surprise insider bundle, and a supply that is spread fairly across sale participants and fully visible on-chain.
