---
title: "Participating in a Launch"
description: "Step-by-step guide for participants to buy tickets and claim tokens on RevolutionPad."
---

You only need a Solana wallet with some SOL and a few clicks. Most wallets (Phantom, Solflare, etc.) connect through a "Connect wallet" button in the top-right of the app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Open the launch page and connect your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the key numbers">
    Check the ticket price in SOL, min/max caps, max tickets per wallet, and start/end time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Buy tickets">
    Decide how many tickets you want within your wallet limit, then click **"Buy tickets"** and approve in your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for settlement">
    Wait for the sale to end; the app will show whether it was a refund, full allocation, or raffle case.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim your tokens">
    If successful, come back to claim your tokens once they are claimable, and enjoy free trading.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  At every step you sign from your own wallet; the app never touches your seed phrase and cannot move funds without an explicit approval.
</Info>
