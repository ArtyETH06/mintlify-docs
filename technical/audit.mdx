## Scope

The audit covered:

- **Token launch lifecycle** — creation, ticket sale, settlement, and refund flows
- **Liquidity management** — pool creation, locking mechanisms, and Meteora integration
- **Access controls** — admin functions, founder permissions, and program authority
- **Token handling** — minting, transfers, vesting schedule setup via Streamflow
- **Edge cases** — minimum raise not met, raffle logic, anti-whale tax calculations
