Your Investor Rank reflects your overall participation and behavior on RevolutionPad. It is based on **Revolution Points (RP)**, which you earn (or lose) through your actions.

## How to Earn RP

### Participation

| Action | RP |
|---|---|
| Participate in a launch | +10 |
| Per ticket purchased | +1 |
| Early participation (first 20%) | +5 |

### Capital Committed

| Action | RP |
|---|---|
| Per 0.1 SOL invested | +1 |
| More than 5 SOL on a single launch | +20 bonus |

### Good Behavior

| Action | RP |
|---|---|
| Hold position > 7 days | +15 |
| Hold position > 30 days | +25 |
| Dump within 10 minutes | -20 |
| Farming pattern detected | -15 |

## The Investor Ladder

| Rank | RP Required | Badge |
|---|---|---|
| Newcomer | 0 – 50 | 🌱 |
| Explorer | 50 – 200 | 🥉 |
| Supporter | 200 – 600 | 🥈 |
| Backer | 600 – 1,500 | 🥇 |
| Builder | 1,500 – 3,000 | 💎 |
| Elite | 3,000 – 7,000 | 👑 |
| Genesis | 7,000+ | 🏛 |
| Legendary | 1 year of activity + flawless behavior | 🧬 |

<Note>
  The **Legendary** rank cannot be reached by points alone. It requires at least one year of continuous activity and no negative flags on your wallet.
</Note>

## Benefits by Rank

Higher ranks unlock progressively better perks:

- **Priority allocation** on oversubscribed launches
- **Access to private launches** reserved for top-tier participants
- **Bonus tickets** on selected launches
- **Fee reductions** on platform operations
