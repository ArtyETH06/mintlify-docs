---
title: "Creator Status"
description: "A separate badge system for founders who launch projects on RevolutionPad."
---

Creator Status is completely independent from the Investor Rank. It recognizes founders who build and ship projects on the platform.

## Creator Badges

| Badge | Requirement |
|---|---|
| 🎨 Creator | 1 project launched |
| 🛠 Builder | 2–3 projects launched |
| 🏗 Serial Creator | 4–6 projects launched |
| 👑 Proven Founder | 3+ successful projects, no rug, clean LP |

<Note>
  Creator badges are permanent. Founders keep their badge even if they don't participate in other launches as investors.
</Note>

## What Counts as "Successful"

A project is considered successful when:

- The raise met its minimum target
- Liquidity was locked as configured
- No rug or malicious behavior was detected
- The token traded normally post-launch

## Benefits

- **Reduced creation fees** for repeat creators
- **Featured placement** in the launch directory
- **Verified badge** displayed on your project pages
