Prestige Titles are rare distinctions awarded automatically based on your on-chain track record. They are stackable with your Investor Rank and Creator Status.

## Available Titles

### 🎯 Sharpshooter

- Global ROI > 2x across 10+ launches
- No farming pattern detected

### 🔥 Alpha Hunter

- Participated in 3+ launches that did > 3x

### 💎 Diamond Hand

- 60%+ of positions held for more than 30 days

### 🛡 Guardian

- 20+ launches with no dump within 24 hours

### ⚡ Early Sniper

- Participated in the first 10% on 15+ launches

### 🧠 Smart Capital

- Average ROI above the platform-wide average

### 🏛️ Quadrian

<Note>
  This is an extremely rare title.
</Note>

- 4x+ average performance on a major launch, **or** top percentile ROI detected
- High TrustScore
- No cluster flags

### 👑 Apex Backer

- 50+ launches participated
- 50+ SOL committed lifetime
- TrustScore > 85
- No toxic behavior flags

## Public Display

Titles are shown publicly on your wallet profile alongside your Investor Rank and Creator Status. All titles are cumulative — you can hold as many as you qualify for.

## Benefits

- **Boosted visibility** on the platform
- **Early access** to beta features
- **Community status** and recognition
