---
title: "Ranks & Titles Overview"
description: "RevolutionPad's internal reputation system rewards participation, project creation, and exceptional performance."
---

RevolutionPad uses three independent, stackable reputation dimensions:

- **Investor Rank** — based on Revolution Points (RP), earned through participation and organic behavior.
- **Creator Status** — a separate badge for founders who launch projects on the platform.
- **Prestige Titles** — rare titles awarded for outstanding performance or behavior.

A single wallet can display all three at once.

> **Example: Backer · Sharpshooter · Proven Founder**
