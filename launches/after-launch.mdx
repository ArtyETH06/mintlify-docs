---
title: "After Launch: Trading and Vesting"
description: "What happens after a successful launch — liquidity, trading, and team vesting."
---

If the min cap is met, the protocol sends the configured token share plus all effective SOL into a couple of locked Meteora liquidity pools (DAMM V2 — 2% base fees — 50% anti-sniper tax).

## Locked Liquidity

- Liquidity is **locked**; the team cannot pull it later, they only earn trading fees.
- Trading starts on that pool as soon as Meteora lists it — **instantly**.

## Team Vesting

Team tokens follow their Streamflow vesting schedule, unlocking gradually over months or years instead of all at once.

<Info>
  Each launch links to its Streamflow vesting contracts, which are fully visible on-chain.
</Info>
