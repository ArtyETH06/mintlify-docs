RevolutionPad takes care of the boring but critical plumbing around your launch. You define supply, splits, and rules once; the protocol handles tickets, settlement, and LP creation on Meteora.

You avoid wiring together launchpad, DEX, and vesting by hand, and you get a structure that investors can read and verify on-chain. Your incentive is simple: **make the token worth trading**, and earn from fees and a slow team unlock instead of a big day-one cash-out.

## Founder Checklist

Before launch you should at least:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define your token purpose">
    Have a clear token purpose and a basic roadmap.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decide your numbers">
    Decide honest numbers for the team and the community.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set ticket price and caps">
    Set a ticket price and caps that match your will.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare vesting">
    Understand your Streamflow vesting schedule and prepare your team wallets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After launch, your job shifts to building: ship features, communicate, and keep liquidity healthy by not fighting the market with side deals and OTC dumps.

> "Respect the price."
