---
title: "Launching on RevolutionPad"
description: "Step-by-step guide for founders to launch their token on RevolutionPad."
---

Founders do a bit more upfront work, but it is still a short checklist.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare your token">
    Create your token on Solana and decide the split between team and sale.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in launch details">
    In the app, fill in launch details: token details, team token supply, ticket price, min/max caps, ~~max winning tickets (used if the max cap is reached)~~, sale window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy the launch">
    Review everything carefully, then deploy the launch and pay the required network fees from your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let the protocol work">
    After a successful raise, let the protocol wire liquidity to Meteora and start trading; you then focus on building and communicating, not on moving pools by hand.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Experienced founders and users can always click through to the on-chain contract, the Meteora pool, and the vesting schedules to verify that the numbers match what the page shows.
</Tip>
